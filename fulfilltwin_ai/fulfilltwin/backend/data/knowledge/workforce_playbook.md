# Labor reallocation
Use cross-trained and currently certified associates before requesting overtime. Protect critical safety, problem-solving, and maintenance roles. Large reassignments should be time-bounded and reviewed against fatigue, ergonomics, and downstream capacity.

# Absenteeism response
At moderate absenteeism, rebalance labor toward the forecast bottleneck and pause low-priority indirect tasks. At severe absenteeism, reduce planned release, sequence work by service risk, and request leadership approval for overtime or voluntary extra time.

# Governance
Operational models must never be used to rank, discipline, or infer the personal performance of individual workers. Recommendations should operate at process, zone, shift, or credential-group level.
