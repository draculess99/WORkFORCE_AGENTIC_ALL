# Energy-aware operations
Shift discretionary charging, HVAC preconditioning, and other noncritical loads away from peak price windows when operational risk is low. Do not curtail safety systems, critical computing, required lighting, or equipment needed for incident recovery.

# Cost trade-offs
Energy savings should be compared against service penalties, labor cost, battery availability, and recovery time. The lowest-energy plan is not necessarily the lowest-total-cost plan.
