# Incident command activation
Open an operational incident when a disruption is likely to create a material service-level breach, safety exposure, or sustained backlog growth. Assign one incident commander, one operations owner, one maintenance owner, and one recorder. Record the trigger, model evidence, rules fired, decision owner, approval status, and next measurement time.

# Human approval
Recommendations that add overtime, reassign a large number of associates, curtail critical equipment, or alter safety-sensitive flow require explicit manager approval. AI outputs are advisory. The application must not execute staffing or safety actions automatically.

# Measurement cadence
Recalculate backlog, throughput, labor availability, equipment capacity, and SLA risk after each material action. During a critical incident, measure at least every fifteen minutes. Close the incident only after the operation has returned to its control band and owners have documented residual risk.
