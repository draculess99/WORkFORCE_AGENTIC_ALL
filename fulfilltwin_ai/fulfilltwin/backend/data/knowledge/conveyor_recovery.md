# Conveyor degradation
When conveyor capacity falls below eighty percent, lower the release rate into affected zones and protect priority work. When capacity falls below sixty percent, activate approved bypass paths, send maintenance to the highest-SLA-exposure constraint, and avoid feeding work faster than downstream teams can clear it.

# Manual bypass safety
Manual bypass lanes require trained associates, clear pedestrian separation, visible work-in-process limits, and supervisor confirmation. Never trade safety controls for throughput recovery.

# Recovery sequencing
Restore the bottleneck that unlocks the greatest amount of protected customer demand. Confirm that upstream release, downstream packing, and dock capacity can absorb the recovered flow before returning to the baseline release rate.
